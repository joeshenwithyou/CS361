Homepage

Welcome to Yu-Gi-GO! This is your home to store a virtual catalog of many of your favorite cards, as well as see how much they are worth.

How to use:

1. Click on the Card Database button to see what cards you want to add to your catalog.

2. When you find a card you want to add, you can choose to add 1, 2 or 3 copies to the catalog.

3. After adding all of your desired cards, click on the My Cards button to view your catalog.

4. In My Cards you can choose to change the rarity of your card, which will update the value.

5. You may remove any card from your library utilizing the delete feature in My Cards.
